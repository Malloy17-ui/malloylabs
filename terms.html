build terms

what you get and what i keep.

plain language. read it once before we start.

what you receive

  • a working version of the indicator, configured to your inputs and use case
  • access via the agreed delivery method — open script, invite-only protected script, or raw .pine file
  • two revision rounds within 14 days of delivery
  • the right to use the indicator on your own charts and accounts, indefinitely

your configured version is yours. i don't take it back, lock you out, or charge you again to keep using it.

what i keep

the underlying logic, components, patterns, and methodologies used to build your indicator remain mine. i retain the right to:

  • reuse them in future builds for other clients
  • reference them in content, posts, and marketing
  • combine them with new logic in original builds i publish under malloy labs
  • build on them, improve them, and evolve them over time

you're paying for a configured solution, not exclusive ownership of the building blocks. exclusivity over a specific component is a separate conversation and a different price.

showcasing your build

by default, when i post about a build publicly — charts, screenshots, methodology breakdowns, results posts — i do so anonymously. your handle, name, account, and identifying details stay out of it.

if you'd like to be tagged or named, you can opt in. opting in is reversible — tell me to stop and i will. posts already published won't be retroactively scrubbed.

what's off-limits to me

  • sharing your trading data, p&l, account details, or context you sent me
  • identifying you publicly without your opt-in
  • selling or licensing your specific configured version to anyone else
  • using anything from our private conversations as content without permission

what's off-limits to you

  • reselling, redistributing, or republishing the indicator under your own name
  • sharing access (invite-only scripts) with people who didn't pay for it
  • reverse-engineering the logic and rebuilding it for resale

use it. trade with it. modify it for yourself if you have the raw file. don't turn around and sell it.

if something goes wrong

the intake doc and scope agreement we sign at the start are the source of truth. if something isn't covered there, we figure it out in good faith.

if you violate the redistribution terms above and i have to revoke access to a protected script, i will. that's why protected scripts exist.

the short version

you get a tool built for how you trade. i keep the right to keep building. your name stays private unless you say otherwise. don't resell my work.

that's it.
